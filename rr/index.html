Railway Resistors

Our company is a manufacturer of raw materials (wire, strip, rod) for electric heating elements from CrAl-base alloys and NiCr-base alloys. On March 2011 we started our own production of grid resistor elements, wire wound resistor elements and ready made resistors assembly, such as a dynamic braking resistors, load and test resistors, neutral grounding resistors, and hoist resistors. As a manufacturer of raw materials and final product, Railway Resistors has a price advantage in comparison with competitors.

EPK Type

Dynamic braking resistor BPRT 63 and BPRT 74 consists of 18 EPK type grid resistors mounted in two rows. They are used in Transmashholding locomotive EP2K. Our company produce EPK type grid resistors for plant Electrovipryamitel (Saransk, Russia).

RLT Type

RLT grid resistors are used as braking resistor in TEP-70 (T), 2TE-116 (T), TEM-7 (S), TEM-18 (S, T), TEM-2 (S), CHME3 (C) locomotives.

          T — Made by Transmashholding plants
          S — Made by Sinara Group plants
          C — Made by ČKD Group plants


LR Type

LR grid resistors are used to motor field-weakening and to limit the current in battery charging circuits and compessor starting circuits in 2TE-10V (T), 2TE-116 (T), TEP-60 (T), 2M-62 (T), TEP-70 (T), TEM-18 (S & T), locomotives.

          T — Made by Transmashholding plants
          S — Made by Sinara Group plants

KF Type

KF type resistors are used as a control, braking and load resistors in low and medium power drivers.

KF type resistors applications are propulsion systems of electric and diesel-electric locomotives, metro coaches, tramways, trolleybuses and cranes.
